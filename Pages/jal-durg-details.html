Sindhudurg Fort

Location: Sindhudurg District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1664

Significance: Built to defend the Maratha coastline from foreign invasions. It is one of the most well-preserved sea forts in Maharashtra and holds a significant place in Maratha history.

Murud-Janjira Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Siddis of Janjira

Year: 15th Century

Significance: Known for its formidable sea defenses and strategic location, this fort remained unconquered during various invasions.

Vijaydurg Fort

Location: Sindhudurg District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1190

Significance: One of the most strategically important forts, it played a key role in controlling the coastline and trade routes.

Suvarnadurg Fort

Location: Sindhudurg District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1660

Significance: Strategically important for controlling the northern sea routes and securing the coastline.

Khanderi Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1670

Significance: Known for its role in controlling the maritime route and its strategic position near the Khanderi island.

Colaba Fort

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Built By: British East India Company

Year: 17th Century

Significance: Located at the entrance of the Mumbai harbor, it played a crucial role in the defense of Mumbai.

Kanakdurg

Location: Near Sangli

Historical Significance: Kanakdurg Fort was used as a military outpost to monitor the surrounding regions. Its location made it a key point for controlling local trade and defense during various historical periods.

Underi Fort

Location: Raigad District, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Year: 1670

Significance: Served as a supporting fort to Khanderi and was part of the strategic defense network along the coast.

Elephanta Fort

Location: Mumbai, Maharashtra, India

Built By: Hindu Kings

Year: 5th-8th Century

Significance: Famous for its rock-cut temples and sculptures dedicated to Lord Shiva, this fort has a rich cultural and historical heritage.

Location: Ratnagiri District, Maharashtra, India
